Why Choose Me

Work experience

Hi and nice to meet you! My name is Irina and I’ve been a makeup artist and hairstylist for over 14 years, and I am a certified beauty professional. My main passion is providing wedding and beauty makeup services, and I am currently based out of Minnetonka, MN. I currently offer airbrush and traditional application services, exclusively using professional-level cosmetic brands to ensure your application is waterproof, long-lasting, and of the highest quality, to give you a flawless look for your special occasion!

Introduction

I love everything about makeup and the beauty industry. What I love most about being a makeup artist is to see the joy a client has when they see their completed makeup look. It is a great satisfaction to me to see someone's self-image boosted or to really feel pretty after they get their makeup done! That part is priceless. My work stands out because my makeup application techniques lend to flawless application, as well as the aspect of creativity and ideas for looks that are unique to me, which I bring to the table.
